{% trans "Our philosophy" %}

{% blocktrans %} GivnTek is designed to help humanity to be able to trade without the help of big actors of e-commerce. We believe in the strength of the local level, even in time of war or network downsizing, and thus bring people from the same community together. We hope that you will find GivnTek convenient and that you will use it for your transactions on a regular basis, at least if you have to do it with computers. {% endblocktrans %}
