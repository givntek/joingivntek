Welcome

GivnTek is a social network for exchanging goods, and choosing how you want to give your objects or take others possessions. Federation allows GivnTek users to join small, trusted bazaars that can connect with one another, and with other ActivityPub services like Mastodon and Pleroma.

Features

Since the project is still in its early stages, the features are growing every day, and there is plenty of room for suggestions and ideas. Open an issue to get the conversation going!
